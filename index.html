Pot Roast
Ingredients:

NY Strip
Salt
Salsa Verde

  1. Take steak out of refrigerator, bring to room temperature (~hour)
  2. Heat cast-iron pan over medium flame
  3. Salt both sides of steak liberally
  4. When pan is preheated (water will evaporate after 3 seconds when dropped into pan), place steak on pan
  5. Cook steak, undisturbed, for three minutes
  6. Flip steak, cook additional three minutes
  7. Remove steak from pan, let rest on plate
  8. After ten minutes, carve steak and serve to guests with a generous side of salsa verde
Ingredients:

Chuck Roll
Salt
Ras el Hanout
Chile Limon
Cracked Pepper
Onions
Garlic, smashed
Thyme, whole sprigs
Wine

  1. Take roast out of refrigerator, bring to room temperature (~hour)
  2. Rub roast with salt and spices
  3. Pour oil in dutch oven, heat until it shimmers
  4. Place roast, fat-side down, into the pot
  5. Brown on all sides, remove from pot
  6. Add onions to pot, cook until transluscent
  7. Deglaze pot with wine, place roast back in
  8. Scatter thyme, garlic; cover pot
  9. Keep at gentle simmer for hours, until roast just gives with the touch of a knife
